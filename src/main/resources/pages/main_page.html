● About us

Qwiz is a platform for creating and taking quizzes. Quizzes do not have a limit for the number of questions or players. Questions can also have infinite numbers of options. Qwiz can be used for educational purposes, fun, or even for hosting game shows. We are looking forward to seeing what you create with Qwiz!

How to play ●

To play a hosted game, you don't need an account! Just enter the game code and your name to join the game. The rest is handled by the host. The built-in ranking system will keep track of your score, and the host can see the results at the end of the game. This way, you can compete against your friends and family!

● Creating a quiz

To create a quiz, you need an account. Accounts are free and easy to create. Once you have an account, you can create quizzes with any number of questions and options.

Development Notice ●

Qwiz is still in development. If you encounter any bugs or have any suggestions, please contact us! We are always looking for ways to improve the platform.
